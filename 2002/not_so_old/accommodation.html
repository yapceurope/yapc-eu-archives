Hotel Reservation

Given that the Octoberfest starts, the minute the conference ends, and 6 million people descend on li'le ol' Munich, to enjoy the local flavour, we suggest you book ASAP (as soon as possible)!

Since hotel reservation around Oktoberfest is not a trivial task, we have commissioned the Conference managing agency CSM to do this on our behalf.

CSM has reserved a large number of hotel rooms in different categories. The reservation service is free of charge. CSM only acts as an agent, so the contract is effected directly between the guest and the hotel. Hotel reservations will require a deposit. Please note, that bookings are only effective with pre-payment of the deposit. Upon payment CSM issues a hotel voucher and sends it along with all necessary hotel information.
