During

  • the infamous auction
  • a raffle of whatever we can't auction
  • speakers dinner - Marienburg
  • p5p dinner
  • bugfixing corner (aim to reduce open bugs < $i_attendees ?)
  • spouses night out (for the forgotten other halves)
  • visit Andechs Kloster Brauerei?

After

  • a walk in the alps - somewhere pleasant
  • visit to Garmisch-Partenkirchen or Berchtesgaden
  • visit to "Cookie Castle"? :)
  • a table at, or at least to visit, the Octoberfest
