Getting to the dinner

Speakers Dinner will be at the restaurant 'Marienburg'

  • from Munich 'Hauptbahnhof' take the S-Bahn 'S8' direction Munich Airport
  • departure is every 20 minutes, hr:12 - hr:32 - hr:52
  • exit the train at station 'Daglfing' (7 stops)
  • exiting the station, head right (Daglfinger Str.)
  • take the first street to the right (Bromberger Str.)
  • take the first street to the right (Gnesener Str.)
  • take the first street to the left (Hohensalzaer Str.)
  • at the end of the Hohensalzaer Str. turn right - there is the restaurant 'Marienburg'
