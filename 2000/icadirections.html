How to get to the ICA

The ICA main entrance in Pall Mall officially opens to the public at 11:00am - obviously for the duration of the conference we need to get attendees in earlier than that.

People need to come to the back entrance of the ICA which is located in Carlton House Terrace to get into the conference each morning.

Once the main doors open people will be able to get in or out through either entrance.

The attached image shows the location of the back entrance.

On the first morning when people register they'll be given a name badge it's vital that people keep hold of it as without security staff will not let people wander in and out of the building.
