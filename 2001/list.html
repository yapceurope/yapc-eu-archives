Mailing List

Any questions about the conference should be directed to the yapc::Europe mailing list, at yapc-europe@lists.dircon.co.uk

  • To receive future messages sent to the mailing list, mail majordomo@lists.dircon.co.uk with "subscribe yapc-europe" in the email body
  • To stop receiving messages, mail majordomo@lists.dircon.co.uk with "unsubcribe yapc-europe" in the email body
  • To change your subscription email address, mail majordomo@lists.dircon.co.uk with "subscribe yapc-europe " in the email body, replacing with your new email address
