Mail::Box
This presentation was given by Mark Overmeer as a three hours tutorial during the YAPC::Europe 2001 conference, which took place August 2 till 4 in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.
The full content of this tutorial is provided "as is", and may be used for your own purpose without notice to the author. However, most slides are only useful in combination with the spoken comment.

YAPC::Europe was organized by the Amsterdam Perl Mongers.
