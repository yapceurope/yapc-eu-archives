Perl/Tk saves your favourite tools
This presentation was given by Mark Overmeer as a three hours tutorial during the SANE2000 conference, which took place from 2 till 4 August 2001 in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.
The full content of this tutorial is provided "as is", and may be used for your own purpose without notice to the author. However, most slides are only useful in combination with the spoken comment.

YAPC::Europe 2001 was organized by the Amsterdam Perl Mongers.
