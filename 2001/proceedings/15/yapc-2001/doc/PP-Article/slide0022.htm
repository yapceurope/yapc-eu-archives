6 Available Translators

At the moment, the following converters are available:

  • pp2html
  • pp2latex
  • pp2sdf
  • pp2cppp

The following chapters explain shortly some of the features of these PerlPoint converters.

pp2html and pp2latex are containted in the PerlPoint::Converters distribution, while pp2sdf and pp2cppp are part of the PerlPoint::Package distribution. Both packages are available on CPAN. pp2html is already in use for real world projects but pp2latex is still alpha software. Nevertheless, the LaTeX file for this article has already been produced with pp2latex.
