5 Documentation

As a PerlPoint converter, it would be great to be delivered with a PerlPoint documentation. If you want to do so, it might be helpful to have a look at the converter packages doc directory. Its parts are intended to be a help to converter documentation authors, describing parts of the base implementation. Feel free to copy these parts and to modify it according to your implementation. (To keep the results maintainable, \INCLUDE may be used to integrate the framework components.)
