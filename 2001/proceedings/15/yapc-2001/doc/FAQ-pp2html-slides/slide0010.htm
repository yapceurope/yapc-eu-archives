How to get numbered or not numbered headlines

Question:

How can I get numbered headlines and how can I suppress the numbering?

Answer:

This can be achieved by the --num_headers option and the --nonum_headers option respectively.

Discussion:

The --nonum_headers option may be used to overwrite the --num_headers option from a predefined style.
