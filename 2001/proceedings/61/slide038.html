Good Names

A good name plus context conveys heaps of information which means LESS DOCS!

Some pointers:

  • Global vs local: $Global, $local

  • Constant vs variable: $CONSTANT, $variable

  • Describe the purpose, not just its contents.

  • Easy to differentiate

  • Consistent

  • Variables with wide scopes should have long, descriptive names

  • Short lived variables can have short names, most of their info comes from context

  • Functions are verbs, variables are nouns.

  • Use context.
