More on Continual Testing

Add a feature, add the docs, add the tests, get it working. Repeat

  • Keeps the scope of bugs small.

  • Very encouraging to have something that works.

  • No "99% done"

  • No swamp of writing tests at the end.

  • Continually high quality

  • Easier to hand off midstream

  • Easier to track progress.
