Geek Health Tips

Personal health:

  • Get some exercise as part of your day. Bike to work.

  • Drink less Coke, eat less pizza.

  • Keep healthier snack food around.

  • Get more sunlight. Work on the roof.

Company health:

  • Stock up with some interesting and healthy food in the vending machines.

  • Install a shower to encourage exercise.

  • Subsidize health club membership only if it gets used.

  • Hire a vegan.
