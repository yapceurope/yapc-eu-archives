Name Calling

Bad names are confusing.

  • Very short: $ob

  • Very generic: $foo, $tmp, $num

  • Redundant: %hash, @array, $object

  • Wrong level of abstraction: $string instead of $name

  • In-Jokes: $TUSK = "Don't say that you want me"

  • Really long: $number_of_days_in_year

  • Hungarian Invasion: $nsirag_Foo

  • Inconsistent
