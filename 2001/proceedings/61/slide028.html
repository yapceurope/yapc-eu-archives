Warnings

KEY CONCEPT HERE

Heed your warnings. Understand and fix them ALL. Again, one broken window.

Seeing a warning should be like seeing a syntax error.

Turn on warnings, strict, etc... leave them on, even in production.

  • Send warnings off to a log file.

  • Separate user mistakes and code mistakes into separate logs to avoid clutter.

PS Turn on taint at the start, you won't get another chance.
