Filling Your Toolbox

Get everything setup nice on your devel machine.

  • Something that doesn't crash.

  • A good editor.

  • Compiler, debugger, libraries, source, docs, FAQs... install everything you need locally.

I'm productive because I rarely write one-offs.

  • Write little single-purpose modules.

  • Higher quality, more attention.

  • Do it up front before it gets messy.

  • Easier to doc, test and install.

  • Simplifies everything

  • Release to CPAN for Fortune and Glory!
