Morale

One of the single most important factors in productivity is morale. A happy programmer is a productive programmer.

Some simple tips:

  • DON'T play mind games

  • An army marches on their stomach, so do programmers.

  • There are some things programmers hate to do. Pay extra for them.

  • Provide bonuses for successful, on-time projects.

  • Encourage high quality.

  • Give reasonable deadlines.

  • Avoid interruptions. Give them some space and privacy.

  • Send them to conferences!
