Mini-Milestones

"How does a project get to be late? One day at a time." -- Fred Brooks

A week is too big to really think about.

Mini-milestones

  • Short. 10 minutes to a day max.

  • Binary. You're done or you're not.

  • Short term. Only work out the milestones for a week or so.

  • Exhaustive. You must milestone all your activities.

  • Adjustable.
