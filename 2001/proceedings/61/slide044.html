Some Style Guide Pointers

Have a style guide, start from perlstyle and salt to taste.

  • Follow the intent, don't sweat unapproved styles

  • The "Who cares?" principle.

        my($self) = shift;      OR
        my $self  = shift;      OR
        my($self) = @_;

    Which is the right style?  WHO CARES!

  • Avoid religion

  • NO TABS!

  • Keep it up to date, keep it under version control.

  • Inmates run this asylum.
