Water Go Down The Hole

The Waterfall Model. It sucks, its popular, you probably use it without realizing.

  • Gather requirements, design, code, test, release.

  • Very inflexible

  • Requires firm requirements up front

  • Nothing has to work until the end.

  • Early mistakes inflate exponentially and are invisible until the end.

  • Has this strange idea that software has a "beginning" and an "end"
