Some Simple Ways To Smash Morale

  • Give impossible deadlines. Continually miss them.

  • Tell them to "just get it done"

  • Use cheesy 'RAH-RAH!' office morale games.

  • Imply a lack of trust.

  • Lie. (We're smart, we'll know)

  • Play management games.

  • Interrupt them a lot. Lots of phone calls and "is it done yet?"

  • Shuffle priorities.

  • Pressure, pressure, pressure!
