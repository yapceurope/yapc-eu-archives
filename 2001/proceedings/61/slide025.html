More on Writing Tests

Tests should be easy, automatic, reflexive and fast.

Use a good test library Test::Simple, Test::More, Pod::Tests...

Your basic unit is the 'ok'.

Keep the scope of testing programs small. Anything over 100 ok's is starting to get too big.

Write dummy testing libraries if necessary. "The Sims"

See also perltest man page.
