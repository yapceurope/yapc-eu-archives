Friction in Computing

"Unforeseen things which will go wrong" -- Von Clausewitz On War

The USMC says it best 

        "Friction may be external, imposed by enemy action, the terrain,
         weather or mere chance.  Friction may be self-induced, caused
         by... a lack of a clearly defined goal, lack of coordination,
         unclear or complicated plans, complex task organizations or
         command relationships or complicated technologies.  Whatever form
         it takes, because war [like programming] is a human-enterprise, 
         friction will always have a psychological as well as physical 
         impact."
              -- USMC Doctrine, "Warfighting" pp 5-6

Sound familiar?
