Assert Yourself

        "We are ready for any unforeseen event that may or may not
         occur."
               - Dan Quayle

Assertions are executable comments. 

        sub everything {
            # We expect life to end with a !
            my $life = make_life;

            ...
        }

instead you can enforce that assumption 

        sub everything {
            my $life = make_life;
            assert( $life =~ /!$/ );

            ...
        }

Catch the error at the point it happens.

Cheap and plentiful. Apply liberally.

They don't replace tests!

See also Carp::Assert
