Recovering

If you decide to finish it...

  • Give a little time off for people to get their heads together.

  • Reduce stress and pressure. Restore morale.

  • DON'T give a new deadline or estimate!

  • CUT FEATURES! Best way to save time.

  • Firm up your requirements.

  • Fix the classic mistakes. Get back to a good build. Restore quality.

  • Cut deadweight, lame programmers and extra management.

  • Institute mini-milestones

Only give an estimate when you're damn good and ready.
