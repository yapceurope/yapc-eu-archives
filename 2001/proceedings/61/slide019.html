Small Subroutines

KEY CONCEPT HERE

If you work in small chunks, life becomes really easy.

Subroutines are for logical units of code.

  • More than just a glorified macro

  • Should be able to describe it in a short sentence.

  • One thought; one paragraph

  • Can be one line up to a few pages of code.
