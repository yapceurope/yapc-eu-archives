Documentation

    "Wanting people to listen, you can't just tap them on the shoulder
     anymore.  You have to hit them with a sledgehammer.  And then
     you'll notice you've got their strict attention."

  • John Doe, "Se7en"

You hate to write docs, but everything needs docs.

Just like testing, document as you go and it won't be so bad.

Embedded docs are good, inline docs are better.

Write inline documentation instead of comments, half the work!

Document private functions 

        =begin _private

        ...

        =end _private

Important things first, incidentals last

Make bad things hard.

Difficult docs means difficult interface
