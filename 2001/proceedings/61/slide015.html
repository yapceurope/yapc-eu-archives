Spiral Models Good

Benefits:

  • Fast releases, about once a week. High visibility.

  • After iteration 1 you have something working. Continual feedback

  • Mid-course adjustments easier. Requirements evolve with the system.

  • Continually high quality. Mistakes caught early.

  • High risk/high cost/low priority features can be left to the end.

Basis for other models: Evolutionary Prototyping, Staged Delivery, etc...
