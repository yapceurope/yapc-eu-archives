Project Recovery

There comes a point when you realize the project might fail to finish at all. At this point you shift into Recovery Mode.

  • Realize you're up sh*t creek. Make sure everyone knows this.

  • Throw out the current schedule. You might not finish at all

  • Reassess. Concentrate on what really needs to be done.

Once everyone's calmed down, you have a two options.

  • Cancel. Saves time, money and probably a few employees.

  • Clean up, cut features, finish.
