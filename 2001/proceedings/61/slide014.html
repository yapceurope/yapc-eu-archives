Spiral Models

_KEY CONCEPT HERE!_

Spiral Development. Like lots of little waterfalls.

  • 1. Gather requirements, design one piece, code, test, release.

  • 2. Adjust requirements, design another piece, code, test, release.

  • Repeat until satisfied or time/money runs out.
